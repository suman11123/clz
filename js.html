Oxford College, Gaindakot

In the year 2000 AD, a new vision was introduced by a group of academic intellectuals and IT experts to address the real needs and demands of the society as well as of the nation, which materialized in the establishment of a pioneer academic institution by the name of Oxford College of Engineering and Management (OCEM) and Oxford Secondary School (OSS) at Gaindakot, nearby Narayangarh city in the central part of Nepal.
